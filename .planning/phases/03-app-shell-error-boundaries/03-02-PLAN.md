Create three-tier error boundary hierarchy (App, Panel, Message) with distinct fallback UIs and wire them into the app shell.

Purpose: Provides error containment at every level so a crash in one message or panel does not take down the entire application. This is the safety net that all future component development relies on.
Output: Three error boundary components, their fallback UIs, comprehensive tests, and wiring into App.tsx and AppShell.tsx.
</objective>
