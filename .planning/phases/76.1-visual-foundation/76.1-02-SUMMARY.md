## Decisions Made
- **Render order fix:** ChatHeader must render AFTER content (FlatList) in the DOM tree for expo-blur to properly update its blur. Fixed by absolute positioning + zIndex 10 on ChatHeader, with content View rendered first.
- **USE_GLASS_COMPOSER toggle:** Module-scope boolean for easy glass/opaque switching during device testing. Defaults to true. D-07 risk documented.
- **20px radius continuity:** Composer keeps existing radii.xl (20px) instead of D-04's 16px to avoid jarring mismatch with pre-existing composer shape. Acknowledged in code comments.
- **HEADER_HEIGHT fallback:** Uses `(insets?.top ?? 44) + 56` -- 44px fallback for pre-layout when safe area insets haven't resolved yet.
- **Content View paddingTop:** Applied to the content wrapper View (not just MessageList) so both EmptyChat and MessageList are offset below the glass header.
