## Decisions Made
- SessionItem subtitle uses caption (12px) not small (13px) -- ChatGPT iOS density match per D-08
- AssistantMessage D-26 override: Soul doc anti-pattern #2 ("No withTiming for appearances") takes precedence over Phase 75's withTiming(150ms) decision

- Typing-begins haptic cooldown set to 2 seconds (prevents haptic fatigue on rapid clear-retype cycles)
- Settings button uses Micro spring scale (0.97) matching other pressable buttons in the app
