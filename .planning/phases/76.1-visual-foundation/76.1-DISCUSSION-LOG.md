## Bard/Gemini Consultation

**Key findings synthesized into decisions:**

1. **Surface colors**: Both Claude and Gemini independently reached same conclusion — keep wider jumps, update Soul doc. "Design specs are hypotheses; the code is the experiment."
2. **Blur performance**: Gemini flagged BlurView + FlatList streaming as high-risk. iPhone 12 and older especially affected. Recommended 55 FPS performance budget with opaque fallback.
3. **Keyboard-blur collision**: Known React Native issue — blur can lag 1-2 frames behind keyboard animation. Prototype early.
4. **Haptic hierarchy**: Gemini correctly identified "haptic fatigue" risk from blanket haptics. Recommended mapping interactions to specific haptic types by importance level.
5. **Rim light technique**: `rgba(255,255,255,0.05)` top-edge border provides more perceived separation than RGB jumps alone, especially on smudged screens.
6. **Quality bar**: "Performance IS a visual feature." Zero-stutter scrolling matters more than RGB compliance.
