**Test:** Tap a session item (selection haptic), open drawer (transition haptic at rest), type first character in composer (transition haptic), tap settings button (tap haptic).
**Expected:** Distinct haptic weights: Light tap on selection, Medium transition on drawer snap/typing-begins, 2s cooldown prevents repeat haptic on rapid clear-retype.
**Why human:** Haptic feel requires physical device with vibration hardware. Cooldown behavior needs real-time testing.

#### 4. Spring animation quality

**Test:** Observe AssistantMessage entrance during streaming vs receiving a completed message. Test settings button press animation. Observe drawer slide animation.
**Expected:** Non-streaming messages have visible translateY+opacity spring entrance. Streaming messages fade in only (no translateY pop-in jitter). Settings button bounces at 0.97 scale. Drawer snaps to rest position.
**Why human:** Spring physics feel (bounciness, settle time, jitter) requires human perception judgment.
